Charity: Water

Ten-year-old Amara used to wake up at 4 AM every day, walking three miles to collect dirty water from a distant well. She missed school constantly and often got sick from the contaminated water her family had to drink.

Today, thanks to a clean water well built just steps from her home, Amara wakes up at sunrise. She turns a simple handle, fills her container with clean water in seconds, and arrives at school on time with a bright smile.

The difference? Clean water gave Amara back her childhood, her health, and her dreams of becoming a teacher.
